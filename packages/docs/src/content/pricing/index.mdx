# Widget Pricing Guide

## Standard Pricing Tiers

### Basic Widget
- **Price**: $99/unit
- **Features**:
  - Standard functionality
  - 1-year warranty
  - Basic support

### Pro Widget
- **Price**: $199/unit
- **Features**:
  - Advanced functionality
  - 2-year warranty
  - Priority support
  - Custom branding

### Enterprise Widget
- **Price**: Contact sales
- **Features**:
  - Full feature set
  - 3-year warranty
  - 24/7 dedicated support
  - Integration services
  - Custom development

## Delivery Options & Timeframes

### Standard Shipping
- 5-7 business days
- $10 per unit
- Free shipping for orders over $500

### Express Shipping
- 2-3 business days
- $25 per unit
- Available for urgent orders

### International Shipping
- 7-14 business days
- Rates vary by location
- Custom clearance assistance available

## Bulk Order Discounts

### Volume Discounts
- 10-49 units: 10% off
- 50-99 units: 15% off
- 100+ units: 20% off

### Annual Contract Discounts
- 1-year contract: Additional 5% off
- 2-year contract: Additional 10% off
- 3-year contract: Additional 15% off

## Payment Terms
- Net 30 available for qualified businesses
- Multiple payment methods accepted
- Volume pricing requires signed agreement
